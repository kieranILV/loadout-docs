# What is Loadout?

Loadout is a game funding platform built on Solana. Developers launch tokens for their games, and traders can buy and sell from day one. Every trade generates fees that go directly into the project treasury to fund development.

No presales. No VC discounts. No unlock schedules creating sell pressure. Just open markets from the start.

## The Problem We Solve

**For Developers:**
Getting funding for indie games is brutal. Publishers take control. VCs want equity. Kickstarter is hit-or-miss. Most developers bootstrap with savings and hope.

**For Traders:**
Crypto gaming has been a graveyard. Inexperienced teams raised huge amounts, and most projects never shipped. By the time retail could buy, VCs were already looking to exit.

## The Loadout Model

We flip the script:

| Traditional | Loadout |
|-------------|---------|
| Private rounds first | Everyone enters together |
| Tokens unlock, creating dumps | No lockups to dump |
| Funding happens once | Funding happens continuously |
| VCs get discounts | Same price for everyone |
| Studio sells tokens to fund | Trading fees fund development |

## Core Principles

### 1. Games First
We're a funding platform for real games. Projects must have demos, documentation, and credible teams. This isn't a meme casino (though trading can get spicy).

### 2. Fair Entry
No whitelists. No private allocations. No "strategic partners" who got in at 10x cheaper than you. The bonding curve treats everyone the same.

### 3. Funding Through Activity
Instead of a studio selling tokens to raise money, trading fees continuously fund development. More attention = more trading = more funding. Incentives align.

### 4. Liquidity Always
Bonding curves mean you can always buy or sell. No waiting for listings. No locked tokens. Real price discovery from trade one.

## Who Is Loadout For?

**Indie & AA Studios** who want funding without giving up control. If you have a game worth building, you can raise capital from people who believe in it.

**Traders & Backers** who want early access to promising games with fair entry. Your trading activity directly funds the games you're backing.

**The Gaming Industry** as a whole. If we can prove this model works at scale, it becomes a new funding rail for game development globally.
