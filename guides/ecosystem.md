# The Ecosystem

Loadout creates a flywheel where traders, studios, and the platform all benefit from the same activity: trading.

<figure><img src="../diagrams/ecosystem-flow.svg" alt="Loadout Ecosystem"><figcaption>The Loadout flywheel</figcaption></figure>

## Key Participants

### 🎮 Studios
Game developers who launch projects on Loadout. They bring:
- Playable demos
- Design documentation
- Team credentials
- Development roadmap

In return, they get:
- Funding without publishers
- No equity dilution
- Built-in community
- Continuous capital from trading

### 💰 Backers
Traders who buy and sell project tokens. They get:
- Early access to promising games
- Fair entry (same terms as everyone)
- Liquidity from day one
- Trading rewards & rebates

Their activity provides:
- Price discovery
- Treasury funding (via fees)
- Market signal for project quality

### 🔧 Loadout Protocol
The platform that connects studios and backers. We provide:
- Launch infrastructure
- Trading mechanics
- Discovery & curation
- Graduation & liquidity

Funded by 0.5% of trading fees.

## The Flywheel

```
Good Game Announced
        ↓
  Traders Buy In
        ↓
  Trading Volume ↑
        ↓
  Treasury Grows
        ↓
  Studio Ships Updates
        ↓
  Token Value ↑
        ↓
  More Attention
        ↓
  More Trading
        ↓
  (repeat)
```

When it works, it compounds. Games that ship updates get more attention, more trading, more funding, more ability to ship. The best projects create virtuous cycles.

## Value Flows

| Flow | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Trader → Token** | Buying creates demand, price rises |
| **Token → Treasury** | 9.5% of every trade funds development |
| **Treasury → Studio** | Funds team, contractors, marketing |
| **Studio → Game** | Ships playable content |
| **Game → Token** | Success increases token value |
| **Token → Trader** | Holders benefit from game success |

## Market-Driven Curation

Loadout doesn't pick winners. The market does.

Projects that capture attention and trading activity accumulate treasury and graduate. Projects that don't, fade. Over time, this creates natural quality sorting without manual gatekeeping.

The cream rises. The noise settles.
