# Graduation

Graduation is the moment a project proves real demand and unlocks deeper liquidity. It happens automatically — no admin action required.

<figure><img src="../diagrams/graduation-flow.svg" alt="Graduation Flow"><figcaption>The graduation mechanism</figcaption></figure>

## How Graduation Works

Projects graduate when their treasury hits **150 SOL** in accumulated trading fees.

That's it. One simple, universal threshold. No team input needed. No arbitrary milestones. Just prove the market cares.

### Why 150 SOL?

| Consideration | Reasoning |
|---------------|-----------|
| **Achievable** | A reasonably hyped project can hit this in days |
| **Meaningful** | ~$20K+ proves real trading interest |
| **Universal** | Same rules for everyone, no gaming the target |
| **Simple** | Easy to understand, easy to track |

## What Changes at Graduation

### Before Graduation
- Trading on bonding curve
- 10% fee on all trades
- 9.5% flows to treasury
- Building toward threshold

### After Graduation
- Meteora DLMM pool created
- Deeper, more efficient liquidity
- Ongoing fee sharing continues
- Increased platform visibility
- LP position permanently locked (fees claimable)

## The Graduation Trigger

Graduation is automatic and on-chain. When the treasury account hits 150 SOL:

1. ✅ Bonding curve trading pauses
2. ✅ Meteora DLMM pool is created
3. ✅ Liquidity is seeded from curve
4. ✅ LP position locked forever
5. ✅ Trading resumes on DLMM
6. ✅ Project marked as "Graduated" on platform

No human intervention. No delays. The market triggers it.

## Post-Graduation Growth

Graduation isn't the end — it's the beginning. Post-graduation:

- **Trading continues** on more efficient AMM
- **Fees keep flowing** to project treasury
- **Visibility increases** on Loadout platform
- **Community grows** as more traders discover the project

The best projects keep building, keep shipping updates, and keep generating trading activity long after graduation.

## FAQ

**Can a project set its own graduation target?**
No. One universal threshold keeps it simple and fair.

**What if a project never graduates?**
Trading continues on the bonding curve. Treasury keeps accumulating. Some projects take longer — that's fine.

**Can graduation be reversed?**
No. It's a one-way door. Once graduated, always graduated.

**What happens to existing holders at graduation?**
Nothing changes for token holders. They can continue trading on the new DLMM pool.
