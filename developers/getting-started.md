# Getting Started

Loadout is built to solve one thing for developers: runway.

You have a game. You need capital to finish it. Publishers want control. VCs want equity. Kickstarter is hit-or-miss. Loadout gives you another option: launch a token, let the market fund you.

## Is Loadout Right for Your Project?

Loadout works best if you have:

- ✅ **A playable demo** — Something people can actually try
- ✅ **Design documentation** — You know what you're building
- ✅ **A team you can show** — Credibility matters
- ✅ **Development roadmap** — Clear plan for funds
- ✅ **Time to ship updates** — The market rewards progress

Loadout probably isn't for you if:
- ❌ You only have a concept (no demo)
- ❌ You want to raise once and disappear
- ❌ You're not comfortable with public accountability

## The Basic Flow

1. **Create your project page** — Demo, docs, team, roadmap, use of funds
2. **Configure your token** — Choose preset, allocations, lockups
3. **Launch** — Trading starts immediately
4. **Accumulate treasury** — 4.75% of every trade funds your project
5. **Ship updates** — Market reprices as you deliver
6. **Graduate** — Hit 150 SOL treasury, unlock deeper liquidity
7. **Keep building** — Treasury keeps growing, game keeps shipping

## What You'll Need

### Required
- Playable demo (hosted or downloadable)
- Game design documentation
- Team information (backgrounds, roles)
- Development roadmap
- Use of funds breakdown
- Solana wallet for treasury

### Recommended
- Trailer or gameplay video
- Screenshots and artwork
- Discord or community channel
- Social presence
- Previous work or portfolio

## Cost

Launching on Loadout is free. No listing fees. No upfront payment.

Loadout makes money from the 0.25% protocol fee on trades. You only pay when trading happens — and that's when you're getting funded too.

## Time to Launch

If you have your materials ready, you can launch in under an hour.

Most of the time is spent on your project page — making sure the demo works, docs are clear, roadmap makes sense. The actual token launch is fast.

## What Happens After Launch

Trading starts immediately. Anyone can buy, anyone can sell. Price is set by the market from trade one.

Your job after launch:
- **Ship updates** — The market rewards progress
- **Communicate** — Post updates, engage with your community
- **Use treasury wisely** — Be transparent about spending
- **Keep building** — The game is what matters

Projects that keep shipping attract more attention, more trading, more funding. Projects that go quiet fade. Simple as that.
